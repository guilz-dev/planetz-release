How it works

Three layers: Cloud, Desktop app, and physical input

Planetz on screen, local LLM on your PC, and Manta for approve-and-talk—one continuous loop for running dev agents.

  1. 01

    Run agents from Planetz

    Open a workspace, assign agents, and decide where human approval is required. Enqueue, monitor, and retry from the app.

  2. 02

    Inference on your PC

    Connect Ollama or other local LLMs. Keep confidential code and docs off the public cloud.

  3. 03

    Approve and speak on Manta

    Answer “may I proceed?” with green or red buttons. Hold Talk to turn speech into a new task instruction in Planetz.

  4. 04

    Scale to Cloud for teams

    Managed private environments for members who cannot run locally—shared workspaces and unified rules.

FAQ

Questions

Can I use Planetz without Manta?
Yes. Planetz Agent Deck runs as a desktop app on its own—you approve and operate from the screen. Manta is optional hardware for safer, more ambient control.
Why use edge AI (Ollama)?
Privacy and security: run inference on your machine without sending source code or internal docs to external APIs. You can keep working offline (e.g. airplane mode) where local models allow.
Is there a discount for hardware owners?
Yes. Manta buyers get Planetz Cloud for $30/mo (regular $40/mo).
What does the Manta LCD show?
Short status text such as IDLE, LISTENING, PROCESSING..., APPROVE?, ERROR—plus ambient light for peripheral awareness.
How do local Deck and Cloud relate?
They share the same deck UX and workflows. Teams can start locally and move workspaces to Cloud without relearning the product.
