Manta specifications

Final concept device for Planetz Agent Deck. Palm-rest form factor with galaxy finish—physical approve/deny, voice orders, and ambient status without living on your main monitor.

Physical controls

Approve and Deny are dedicated buttons on the left wing—one thumb, clear intent.

Controls & surfaces

Part Role Experience
Status LCD (center) Short text/icons for current state. Glance at Manta to know what the squad is doing.
Ambient ring (cyan edge) Color and pulse encode state in peripheral vision. Feel squad motion before you touch the device.
Approve (green ✓) Confirm proceed. One thumb when the agent asks.
Deny (red ✗) Reject or stop. Clear intent for risky operations.
Talk (PTT mic) Hold to speak; host STT → Planetz instructions. Skip long typing for orders.
Side dial (textured) Scroll diffs / switch sessions. Rotate through parallel work.
Galaxy finish + Planetz mark Brand and product story on the desk. Command from your palm.

Connectivity & stack

Heavy inference runs on your PC (Ollama, etc.). Manta is an I/O appliance over USB-C or BLE.

  • USB-C (bus power) — primary
  • BLE — optional wireless desk setup
  • HID approve/reject — driverless
  • Host app — Planetz Agent Deck (desktop)
